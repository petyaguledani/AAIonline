Digital Attachment History Assessment (DAHA)

Welcome to the Digital Attachment History Assessment

This assessment helps you explore how your early relationships may influence your current relationship patterns.

You'll be guided through a series of questions about your childhood experiences and early relationships. You can take your time with each question, and the system may ask you follow-up questions based on your responses.

Important Information

  • This assessment takes approximately 45-60 minutes to complete.
  • You can pause and resume at any time by using the save button.
  • Your responses are encrypted and kept private.
  • Some questions may bring up emotional memories - take breaks as needed.

Consent

By continuing, you acknowledge that:

  • This assessment is for personal insight and is not a diagnostic tool.
  • Your data will be stored securely and used only for the purposes you consent to.
  • You can request to delete your data at any time.
